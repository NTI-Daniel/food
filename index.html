Welcome to Drizzys Kitchen

Drizzys Kitchen has throughout the years been a loyal and very popular restaurant in Sweden. Many people come here to try our very delicious Beef, that is slowcooked by Drizzy who has been trained of Gordon Ramsay himself.

Here at Drizzys you will always have something to do, not hungry? No problem, come and take a dring at our very beautiful bar that has several bartenders that are trained just to satisfy your needs.

At night times this kitchen turns in to the best bar in town, where you can do everything from just sitting down and watching the game. To playing shuffleboard with your friends.
